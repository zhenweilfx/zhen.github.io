Q1 Who are you walking with?

As you continue walking in the woods with someone, you will meet an animal.

Q2 What is the animal?

Going deeper into the forest, I found a vacant lot. At the center of it is a house.

Q3. How big is the house you found? Is there a fence in the house?

Psychological test results

Q1. Walking in the woods is the most important person in my life

Q2. The size of the animals I met indicates the size of the problem I have. If the animal is vigilant and inaccessible, you are a positive person. On the contrary, if you have a very peaceful atmosphere, you will tend to be passive.

Q3. The size of the house shows the strength of ambition . The presence or absence of a fence indicates whether you have an open personality. Those who do not have a more straightforward personality.
